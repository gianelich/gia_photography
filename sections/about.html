HEY, I'm Gia.

Low-budget photographer. Lover of freedom and good stories. Currently residing in San Juan, Argentina. If you want to know a little bit more about myself (at your own risk), keep reading.

I've always had a natural interest for photography. My journey started when I was 10 years old where I wanted to take photographs of the consequences of the climate change in the world. Yeah, I know. Years went by until one day that I found out about a great photography course for beginners. I basically learnt how to handle my DSLR and the rest is history. I got a degree on Business Administration in UADE University at Buenos Aires and then did courses on UX/UI Design and Web Development. Being a UX Designer is the direction that I am taking to satisfy both my creative and analytic sides; and my passion for photography of course. One thing is for sure: my main inspirations are people and nature. And getting experiences is what gets me going. But this is not about me. If you are ready to tell your story, let me know.
